Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about SkillRise.

Signing up for a course is easy! Simply browse our course catalog, select the course you're interested in, and click the "Enroll Now" button. If you don't have an account yet, you'll be prompted to create one before completing your enrollment.

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, and Apple Pay. For certain courses, we also offer payment plans through Affirm.

Most of our courses provide lifetime access, allowing you to learn at your own pace. Some specialized courses or bootcamps may have a specific timeframe for completion. The course details page will clearly indicate any time limitations.

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for most courses. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, you can request a refund within 30 days of enrollment. Some exceptions may apply for certain promotional offers or bundled courses.

Our support team is available 24/7 to help with any technical issues. You can reach us through the "Help" button in your account dashboard, by emailing support@skillrise.com, or by calling our support hotline at +1 (800) 555-0124.
